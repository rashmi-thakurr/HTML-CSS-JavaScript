BROKEN BIRD

Pieces of species,
That couldn't be joined
"Broken Bird"
The name was coined,
Shattered wings-damaged bird,
Flight of which,
Seemed so absurd

Fell down the sky,
No breathe, no sigh,
Collapsed on earth,
Left of no worth

Even then a denial,
And a desire to fly,
Led fortitude to amplify,
What it did?
Stood for a while
All it did,
A denial and smile.
