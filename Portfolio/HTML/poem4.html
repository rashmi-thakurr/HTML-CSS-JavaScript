HOW THIS POEM'LL GO

When I sat to write
This interplay of words and rhythm
Or to fly this lyrical kite
Or to play with illusionary prism
I didn't know
Where musical winds will blow
I didn't know
How this poem will go

my ideas ,my thoughts, my notions
The petals of my flowering emotions
That tenanted my body and head
Not one or ten but hundred
Some lively while others dead
Be it completely grown
Like a decaying fleshless bone
Or a developing new bud
As flood of a young blood
Magenta, violet, velvet or red
Oh I understand that
From this thoughtful rainbow
I had to chose one glow
But, I didn't know
How this poem will go

You name it,
Anything that your brain emit
A mystery , a tragedy,
Or hunger well fed by money
Or for instance
That raindrop eternal dance
Or about a ruined chance
Or affection, love and romance
Portraying exchanged glance
Something suspicious or thriller
My poem as a story teller
Or about my mother's smell
Or my fondness for father
Or a thing that ring humour bells
Different themes that I could gather
Which one to chose
And the one I use
To all left, a big no
All were the arrows and me one bow
Yes, I wanted my feelings to flow
Again, I didn't know
How this poem will go
